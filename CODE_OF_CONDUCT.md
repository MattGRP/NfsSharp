# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Project participants are expected to communicate respectfully, accept constructive feedback, focus discussions on technical outcomes, and avoid harassment or discriminatory behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, personal attacks, threats, deliberate disruption, and publication of another person's private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when necessary to protect the project community.

This policy is based on the principles of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
